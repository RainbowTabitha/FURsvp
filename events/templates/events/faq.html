Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FURsvp

General Questions

FURsvp is a free tool designed for event organizers to gauge attendance prior to their events. It helps you create, manage, and track RSVPs for your community gatherings in a safe and organized way.

Yes, FURsvp is completely free to use for both event organizers and attendees. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

For Group Organizers

To create an event, you'll need to be an approved organizer. Once approved, click the "Create Event" button in the navigation bar. Fill out the event details including title, date, location, and any specific requirements. You can also set capacity limits and add custom questions for attendees.

To become an approved organizer, you'll need to be verified by our moderation team. This helps ensure the safety and quality of events on the platform. Contact us at info@fursvp.org to start the approval process.

No, you can only post events for the state in which the event is physically located in, in the instance that the state is for the same state as the FURsvp instance. This restriction helps ensure:

  • Better local event management
  • Compliance with state-specific regulations
  • More accurate event information
  • Proper verification of event details

Important: Attempting to post events for states other than where your event is located may result in account suspension or termination and / or removal of your event.

Once your event is created, you can manage it from your profile page. You can view RSVPs, send messages to attendees, update event details, and cancel or reschedule if needed. You can also assign assistants to help manage the event.

To assign assistants to your group:

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Click on the "Manage Assistants" button
  3. Use the search field to find users by name or username
  4. Click "Add" next to the user you want to assign

Assistants can help you:

  • View and manage RSVPs
  • Send messages to attendees
  • Update event details
  • Monitor event capacity

Note: You can remove assistants at any time from the same management page.

For Attendees

Browse the events page to find an event you're interested in. Click on the event to view its details, then click on a status under RSVP. You can choose to RSVP, not attend, or maybe attend. You may need to answer some questions or provide additional information depending on the event requirements.

Yes, you can cancel your RSVP at any time before the event. Go to your profile page, find the event in your RSVPs, and click the "Cancel RSVP" button. It's courteous to cancel as soon as possible if you know you can't attend.

You'll receive notifications for any changes to events you've RSVP'd to, including updates to the time, location, or if the event is canceled. Make sure your notification settings are enabled in your profile.

Safety and Privacy

If you encounter any issues or violations of our terms of service, please contact our moderation team at info@fursvp.org. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly. Please do not report events that are not in violation of our terms of service, as this will be considered abuse of the reporting system. Additionally, please do not take situations into your own hands and report them to the moderation team, as this will be considered abuse of the reporting system.

We take privacy seriously. Your personal information is only shared with event organizers when you RSVP to their event. You can control what information is visible on your profile through your privacy settings.
