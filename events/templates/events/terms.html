Terms of Service

Last Updated: June 17, 2025

Overview

FurSVP is a free tool designed for event organizers to gauge attendance prior to their events. It is intended to be a safe, inclusive, and respectful space for all users to share and coordinate community gatherings.

1. Events

a. Non-Discrimination

Events may not discriminate or promote discrimination on the basis of:

  • •Race or color
  • •Gender or gender identity
  • •National origin or ancestry
  • •Age
  • •Disability or genetic information
  • •Religion or belief systems
  • •Political beliefs

b. Safety and Legality

Events may not promote or encourage:

  • •Illegal activity
  • •Dangerous or reckless behavior
o

c. Prohibited Content

Events may not be used for:

  • •Organizing hate groups
  • •Bullying, harassment, or targeting individuals or groups

d. Age-Restricted Content

  • •Events that are NSFW (Not Safe For Work) or sexually explicit must be clearly marked as 18+.
  • •Events that include unattended alcohol service must be labeled as 21+.

e. Visual Restrictions

Event listings must not contain:

  • •Nudity
  • •Hate symbols
  • •Fetish wear

2. User Accounts

a. Conduct and Representation

Accounts may not be used to:

  • •Promote hate speech or discriminatory content
  • •Encourage illegal or dangerous behavior

b. Profile Content

Profile pictures, bios, and display names must not:

  • •Contain nudity or sexually explicit imagery
  • •Display hate symbols
  • •Include fetish gear or content inappropriate for general viewing

3. Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • •Removal of event listings
  • •Suspension or termination of user accounts

We reserve the right to modify or update these terms at any time. Continued use of FurSVP implies acceptance of any changes.

If you have questions, feedback, or concerns about a listing or user, please contact our moderation team at info@tabithahanegan.com.
